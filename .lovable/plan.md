

## Kid-Friendly YouTube Video Gallery

A fun, colorful single-page video gallery designed for kids with large, easy-to-tap video cards and popup playback.

### Visual Design
- **Bright, playful color scheme** with primary colors (red, blue, yellow, green)
- **Rounded corners** on all cards for a friendly, soft look
- **Large video thumbnails** (2-3 per row) making it easy for kids to browse and tap
- **Fun hover effects** like gentle bounce or glow when hovering over a video
- **Simple, readable layout** with no distracting elements

### Core Features

#### 1. Video Grid
- Responsive grid layout with large thumbnail cards
- Each card shows the YouTube video thumbnail pulled automatically from YouTube
- Cards have colorful borders or backgrounds
- Smooth scrolling for browsing through 20+ videos

#### 2. Popup Video Player
- Click/tap any video to open a centered modal popup
- Video starts playing automatically in the popup
- Large, easy-to-find close button (big X or "Close" button)
- Popup has a fun colored border matching the site theme
- Click outside or press close to stop and dismiss

#### 3. Kid-Friendly UX
- No complicated navigation — just scroll and click
- Visual feedback when hovering/tapping cards
- Mobile-friendly for tablet use

### Technical Notes
- Fully static — no backend needed
- Videos will be defined in a simple array (easy to update)
- YouTube embeds will be responsive and work on all devices
